☽ the artist

Hi, I'm Echo Moon Art

Character illustrator, chronic doodler, professional raging fruit basket.

I paint characters the way I'd want to meet them: a little witchy, a little soft, always dramatic about it. Every piece starts as a doodle in a sketchbook margin and ends up somewhere much stranger.

I'm drawn to the in-between: the moment before a spell finishes casting, the last breath before a confession, the version of someone that only shows up in low light. If a piece feels like it's holding its breath, I've done my job.
